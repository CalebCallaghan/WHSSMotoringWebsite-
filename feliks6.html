Koenigsegg Agera RS

Koenigsegg is a swedish high performance sports car company. It was founded in 1994 by Christian von Koenigsegg who had a dream to build the best sports car. The first Koenigsegg car, the CC8s was released in 2003.

The Koenigsegg Agera RS was released in 2015. It has a 5.0 liter twin turbo V8 engine and it has 1176 horsepower. Only 25 units will ever be released and the car costs over a million dollars. The fuel economy is 21L/100km city and 18L/100km highway.

One of the good things about it is that it is one of the fastest cars in the world. The car has also broken lots of world records. Therefore making it the car to have right now.

A bad thing about it is that it is a rare car with only 25 units made so most people will not be able to own one. All 25 of them were custom ordered and are all unique.

The car is obviously not recommended for students because it is an expensive rare sports car that will be expensive to buy and maintain. As well as it is currently illegal in all of North America.
