Aprilia RSV4

Aprilia was founded in 1945 by Cavaliere Alberto Beggio. They started producing motorcycles at the end of the 60’s. They became more popular in the 70’s.

The Aprilia RSV4 was released in 2009. One unique thing about the bike is that it has a V4 engine whereas most other sports bikes have inline 4 engines. The RSV4 is a slimmer bike and you can have your legs tucked in more and it makes the bike feel lighter. The top speed of the bike is 286 km/h.

The wet weight of the bike is 179 kg and the fuel capacity is 18.5 litres. The engine is 999.6 cc 65-degree V4.
