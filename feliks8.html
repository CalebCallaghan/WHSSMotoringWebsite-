Tesla Model 3

Tesla Motors was founded in 2003 by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning. It was given funding by Elon Musk in 2004 and he became the chairman of the company. They introduced their first electric car, the Tesla Roadster in 2006. It was later renamed to just Tesla to show that they donâ€™t only make cars.

The Tesla Model 3 was released in 2018. It is completely electric and does not use any gas to run. It has a big battery inside of it. The standard range version gets 354 km and the long range one can go 523 km on a single charge. It has a huge touchscreen in the middle to do most of the things, instead of having buttons.

One of the very cool features of the Model 3 is the full glass roof that it has. Also, it can reach a top speed of 140mph. Another neat feature is Tesla Autopilot which can keep you in your lane and can control the braking too. It also has a built in charging dock for Apple and Android phones.

The car is not recommended for students because it is expensive and uncommon, which can make running it very expensive.
