Ford Fiesta

Ford is a car company that was founded in 1903 and named after its founder, Henry Ford. In 1919, Edsel Ford (son of Henry Ford) became president of the company. They make cars, trucks, and tractors. The first car they put out was the Model T and it was very successful. They built new factories to produce the Model T.

The first Fiesta was introduced in 1976 and over one million were sold in the first year. Right now, the car usually comes with a 1.6L engine and an average fuel economy of 7L / 100km. It is one of the best selling cars in the UK. The newest model is the Ford Fiesta Mk8.

One of the good things about the Ford Fiesta is the high fuel economy / miles per gallon which means you will save lots of money on fuel compared to other larger size vehicles. Another good thing about the car is that the price is affordable. It also has good safety features and it is reliable. The car is fun to drive and has very good steering and handling.

One of the problems with the Fiesta is the small backseat space and small cargo space. Another one of the problems is that it is not as powerful as other cars, so it will not speed up as quickly especially going up hills. Another problem is some of the automatic transmission fiestas have problems with their transmission with it constantly breaking again even after being fixed, but the manual transmission ones do not seem to have the problem.

I rate this as a very good car for students because it has good fuel economy so you will save money on gas.
