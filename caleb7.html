Speed depends again on the make and what not. The newer model are more commonly 4 stroke. Why You Buy A Newer Model?

If your trying to be more green the newer models are the way to go. 4 strokes produce less carbon emissions and thus hurt the environment less. Overall there more family friendly and are way safer. So for young adults or older teenagers driving one for their first time the newer models are perfect.
