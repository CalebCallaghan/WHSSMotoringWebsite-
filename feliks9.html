Chevrolet Spark

The Chevrolet company was founded in 1911 by William Durant. William had a partnership with a race car driver named Louis Chevrolet and he named his company after him. Two of their first cars was the Royal Mail roadster in 1914 and Baby Grand touring car in 1914.

The Chevrolet Spark is a very small car and was originally called the Daewoo Matiz. The first generation of the Daewoo Matiz came out in 1998. It came out in the U.S. and Canada in 2013. The newest one is the fourth generation starting from 2016. The engine is either a 1.0 L or 1.4 L and it is available as a sedan or hatchback.

One of the good things about the Spark is the fuel economy is very good. It is 8.1 L/100km in the city and 6.2 L/100km on the highway. Another good thing is the visibility it provides for the drivers.

The bad thing about the car is that the engine is very weak at 98 hp. When it is full of passengers and stuff in the back it can be slow to accelerate. Also, merging into the highway will be harder because it doesn’t have alot of horsepower.

This car should be good for students because its small and has good fuel economy. It’s also cheap and affordable for a low budget.
