The first time I rode a dirt bike

My experiences

It's amazing riding a dirt bike. You get the rush of adrenaline and you feel the wind on your body and you can see everything around you just passing by. It is a great way to start your summer. When you first pull out your bike and make sure it's ready to ride, you check it over to see if anything needs to be fixed, you make sure your gas tank is full and you check everything else. If everything is good to go you feel that rush, the rush before you even get to ride. The rush that you get through your body when you realize that you get to ride, that rush is one of the greatest feelings in the world.

When I first rode last year I fell and I didn't fall off to the point where I was on the ground I fell into a hydro pole after a tree branch hit me in the face, the tree branch hit my helmet and it caught me off guard and then I fell over. And of course my boyfriend was recording me and he couldn't stop laughing at me. This year I will be riding some more.
