Roost protector

Buying a roost protector. Roost protector should fit properly on your chest back and arms. Depending on the roost protector they can go either under or over your jersey. A roost protector is one of the most important protections you should buy. The roost protector makes sure when your following behind someone you don't get rocks and other hard objects thrown at our chests.it also protects you if you fall off your bike your chest and back wonâ€™t get scratched. Neck brace.

Buying the proper neck brace should fit snugly around your neck. Once your helmet is on your head the neck brace should not move like it shouldn't lift up. The neck brace shouldn't be bigger. It should have contact with your back and chest. The neck brace is used for if you fall off your bike it makes sure your neck doesn't bend weird ways or break. The neck brace is the most important thing to buy for dirt bike protection. Neck brace cost anywhere from $297.99 - $459.99 and higher

Skid plate for your bike

Buying a skid plate foy your bike. The skid plate goes underneath your bike and it protects the bottom of your bike from getting scratched and damaged. The skid plate protects the cases ( the bottom end of the engine) from cracking from rocks when your riding. Can also protector the frame of your bike. The skid also protects your shift lever and brake lever from bending and breaking. Skid plates cost anywhere from $99.99 - $150.00 which is cheaper than replacing the bottom end of the motor.

If you have a 2 stroke you might want to invest in a pipe guard as well it will save your pipe from dents ,bends, cracks and splits which will reduce your horse power and throttle response.
